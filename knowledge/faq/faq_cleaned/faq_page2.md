## My fan is spinning at full speed!

## There is no video on my HDMI monitor

Ensure the monitor is connected and powered _before_ powering on the Apollo. :contentReference[oaicite:19]{index=19}

## My node only has 10/64 connections

This is normal during sync — it may take several days. :contentReference[oaicite:20]{index=20}

## I can’t connect to Wi-Fi

Use a 2.4 GHz network; reconnect to that band for reliability. :contentReference[oaicite:21]{index=21}

## My node shows errors during sync

Node syncing is resource-intensive, and temporary errors can occur. :contentReference[oaicite:22]{index=22}
